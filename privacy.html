Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Your privacy matters to us. Fahamni collects only the data necessary to provide our services and never sells your personal information to third parties.

📄 1. Introduction

Welcome to Fahamni ("we," "our," or "us"). Fahamni is a mobile application that connects students with qualified tutors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our app.

By using Fahamni, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

📦 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we access your device location to show nearby tutors and relevant content.
  • Camera: With your permission, we access your camera to allow profile photo uploads and in-session media sharing.
  • Microphone: With your permission, we access your microphone to enable voice features during tutoring sessions.
  • Storage: We access device storage to save and retrieve media files used within the app.
  • Notifications: We send push notifications for session reminders, messages, and updates.
  • Usage Data: Anonymized data about how you interact with the app, used to improve the experience.

🔗 3. Google Services

Fahamni uses the following Google services:

  • Google Sign-In: To authenticate users securely without storing passwords.
  • Google Maps: To display maps and help students find tutors near their location.

Our use of information received from Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

🎯 4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Fahamni platform
  • To match students with suitable tutors
  • To enable communication between students and tutors
  • To send session reminders and relevant notifications
  • To ensure platform security and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

🤝 5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We may share data only in these limited cases:

  • With service providers who help operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • With your explicit consent

🗄️ 6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

⚙️ 7. Your Rights & Controls

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw location, camera, microphone, or notification permissions anytime via device settings
  • Revoke Google Sign-In access via your Google account settings

🔒 8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

👶 9. Children's Privacy

Fahamni is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

🔄 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

✉️ 11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
